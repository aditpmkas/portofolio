RATURU: Home Fever

HOME 3S QUEST RATURU REDRUSE

Released: 2025

Work with: Buzzy Wizzy Studio

Platform: PC

Team Size: 10

Project Length: 1 week (Game Jam)

Responsibilities: Programmer, Level Designer (Level 1), Story Contributor

Engine and Tools: Unity

Summary of Contributions

As a programmer and level designer, I contributed to the development of Level 1 for RATURU: Home Fever during a 1 week game jam. I also collaborated with the team on the game's story, focusing on creating an engaging and challenging experience within the given time constraints.

RATURU Overview

Aspect 1

This GIF shows the character waking up from a dream with a shocked expression, only to fall asleep again, setting the stage for the dream-like nightmare.

Aspect 2

After falling asleep again, the character reawakens in a dream, but their room has transformed, featuring multiple doors. This GIF highlights the environment change mechanic I implemented.

Aspect 3

This GIF shows the universal 'unknown place' where the player is teleported after entering any door. I implemented the logic for this teleportation and the subsequent return to the room, with clues appearing if the correct door was chosen.

Aspect 4

This GIF showcases the clue system I developed, where entering correct doors triggers a unique sound and reveals new clues on the wall to guide the player through the dream.

Aspect 5

This GIF displays the exit door. I programmed this door to remain locked until the player successfully enters three correct doors, providing a clear objective and progression through the level.
