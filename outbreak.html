Summary of Contributions

Outbreak was developed as a group project for the "Game Intelligence" subject during a game development bootcamp at my campus. Our team of three built the game in just one week. My main responsibilities were game design and programming. I focused on implementing the AI for the zombies, including navigation and state changes (idle, chase, attack, death), as well as other core gameplay mechanics.
