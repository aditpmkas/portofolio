Urban Night City - Tuti and Agus

Released: 2024

Work with: Group Project

Platform: Video

Team Size: 4

Project Length: 1 month

Responsibilities: 3D modeling, animation, editing

Engine and Tools: Blender

Assignment: 3D Computer Graphic and Animation subject, Semester 4, President University

Summary of Contributions

This 3D animation project was created as an assignment for the "3D Computer Graphic and Animation" subject in my 4th semester at President University. Our group of four worked on the project for one month. My main responsibilities were 3D modeling, animation, and editing. Here are some of the specific tasks I handled:

  • Lamp with flickering effect
  • Traffic light
  • Trash can with cigarette
  • Apartment modeling
  • Idle character animation
  • Credit scene environment set
  • Shophouse modeling
  • Shophouse items

Award: FINPROTION 2025 Winner

Our group project "Urban Night City" was awarded as the Winner of FINPROTION 2025 (Final Project Appreciation) by the Faculty of Informatics, President University. This recognition was given for outstanding creativity, innovation, and dedication in the Final Project for 3D Computer Graphics & Animation (Batch 2023, Semester 4).
